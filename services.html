Our B2B Services

Complete textile manufacturing, processing & supply solutions for garment manufacturers and fabric distributors.

01

Bulk Fabric Supply

Competitive wholesale pricing on cotton shirting, suiting, woven & printed fabrics. Flexible payment terms with minimum orders from 500 meters. Consistent quality across batches guaranteed.

02

Custom Dyeing

Precision reactive dyeing with Pantone/TPX color matching. Grade 4-5 wash and light fastness. Over 200 standard shades plus custom color development for your brand requirements.

03

Fabric Printing

Digital printing for photo-quality unlimited-color designs. Rotary and screen printing for bulk orders. All techniques deliver vibrant, wash-resistant results on cotton and blended fabrics.

04

Fabric Processing

Comprehensive post-production services: thread rolling, fabric brushing, calendering, mercerizing, peach finishing, enzyme washing, and anti-shrink treatments at our Lower Parel facility.

05

Sample Development

Rapid prototyping and sample creation within 5-7 working days. Lab-dip matching, strike-off approvals, and pilot production runs before committing to bulk manufacturing orders.

06

Pan-India Delivery

Reliable logistics network covering all major textile hubs — Surat, Delhi, Bangalore, Tirupur, Kolkata. 7-15 day delivery with real-time tracking. Export shipping also available.
