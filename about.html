Our Journey

From a vision to India's trusted textile partner

Founded on 27th November 2013, Arama Tessile LLP began with a clear mission — to provide the highest quality cotton fabrics to India's garment manufacturing industry. Starting from our registered office at Kalbadevi Road in Mumbai's historic textile district, we grew steadily through an unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Today, with our state-of-the-art processing facility at Kewal Industrial Estate in Lower Parel and an annual turnover of ₹25-100 Crore, we serve hundreds of garment manufacturers across India and have established a growing export presence worth $0.55M annually. Our journey from a small LLP to a major textile supplier reflects the trust our clients place in us.
